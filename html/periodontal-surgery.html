Treatment Options for Every Situation

  • Bone Grafting

    Bone Grafting brings back the natural structure of your face by replacing lost bone.

  • Crown Lengthening

    Crown Lengthening exposes more of your tooth, often in preparation of a restoration, by adjusting the gum and bone levels.

  • Gum Grafting

    Gum Grafting restores the gum tissue to a natural level and position against the teeth by adding tissue grafts to areas where it is needed.

  • LANAP®

    LANAP® eradicates disease-causing bacteria with a technologically-advanced, gentle laser treatment that leaves healthy tissues behind to heal and reconnect to the teeth.

  • Ridge Augmentation

    Ridge Augmentation after tooth loss recreates the natural contour of your gums by adding gum or bone grafts to the areas where they are needed.

  • Sinus Lifts

    Sinus Lifts are typically performed following tooth loss to prepare your mouth for tooth replacement by adding bone to your upper jaw, on either side of your nose, when there is not enough bone height.
